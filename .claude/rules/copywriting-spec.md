# Rule: Alex Hormozi Copywriting Spec & Direct Response Architecture (`Vespo.io`)

When generating, editing, or auditing any text, headlines, descriptions, or call-to-actions (CTAs) for Vespo (`vespo.io`), you MUST strictly adhere to Alex Hormozi's direct-response frameworks ($100M Offers, $100M Leads, $100M Money Models) and avoid all generic AI marketing buzzwords.

## 1. The Core Niche & Target Audience
- **Target Client**: Independent **Austin Food Trucks** & local independent restaurants (Rainey Street, South Congress, East Austin food truck parks, Barton Springs).
- **Client Psychology**: Time-starved, exhausted, hands-on food entrepreneurs who hate complicated tech, hate 30% third-party delivery commissions (DoorDash/UberEats), hate slow 15MB PDF menus in the 103° Texas heat, and want to capture high-margin ($3,000–$10,000+) corporate/wedding catering bookings.

## 2. The $100M Offers Framework (The Value Equation)
Every headline, feature card, and section MUST maximize the 4 pillars of the Value Equation:
$$\text{Value} = \frac{\text{Dream Outcome} \times \text{Perceived Likelihood of Achievement}}{\text{Time Delay} \times \text{Effort \& Sacrifice}}$$

1. **Dream Outcome (Maximize)**:
   - *Wrong (Fluffy)*: "Elevate your digital dining presence."
   - *Right (Hormozi)*: "Packed lines down the block. Zero 30% DoorDash commission loss. Automatic $5,000+ corporate catering inquiries in your inbox."
2. **Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (Maximize)**:
   - *Right*: Use concrete social proof, local Austin familiarity, and visual side-by-side proof (e.g., "Built specifically for mobile—how 94% of food truck customers order").
3. **Time Delay (Minimize to Zero)**:
   - *Wrong*: "Custom development timeline."
   - *Right*: "Live on the street in 14 Days Flat right before the weekend rush."
4. **Effort & Sacrifice (Minimize to Zero - Zero Friction)**:
   - *Wrong*: "Our collaborative onboarding portal."
   - *Right*: "You stay focused on the griddle. Send us your Instagram menu pics and location schedule—we handle 100% of the tech, hosting, and mobile optimization."

## 3. Mandatory Offer Enhancers: Guarantee & Scarcity
- **The Risk Reversal Guarantee**: Always prominently feature the **100% Risk-Free Preview Guarantee**: *"We build an interactive, working mockup of your new food truck website before you pay a single dollar. If you don't love the preview, you walk away with zero obligation."*
- **Scarcity & Urgency**: *"To maintain dedicated live-schedule syncing and VIP Austin support, we only partner with 4 Austin food trucks per month."*
- **Self-Funding ROI Framing ($100M Money Models)**: *"Just ONE corporate catering gig or 15 extra weekend lunch orders pays for your entire website refresh."*

## 4. Anti-AI Buzzword Prohibition
You are STRICTLY PROHIBITED from using the following fluffy, passive, or generic AI buzzwords anywhere on the site:
- `Elevate`, `Unleash`, `Synergy`, `Seamless`, `Cutting-edge`, `Next-level`, `Revolutionize`, `Empower`, `Bespoke`, `Holistic`, `Unlock`, `Crafting`, `Digital realm`.
- *Rule*: Write at a **Flesch-Kincaid Grade 6 Reading Level**. Use punchy verbs, short sentences, and concrete numbers (`14 days`, `0% commission`, `0.3s load time`, `$5,000+ catering`).

## 5. Live Competitor & Search Teardown Protocols (`fetch` & `brave-search`)
- When asked to overhaul copy, use the `fetch` MCP tool to inspect competitor or old restaurant copy, or use `brave-search` to find live Austin food truck customer complaints, and instantly convert those real-world frustrations into high-converting bullet points.
